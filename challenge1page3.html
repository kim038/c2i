Capilano Suspension Bridge

The Capilano Suspension Bridge is named after its iconic bridge, which measures 140 meters long and hangs 70 meters above the Capilano River. Beyond the bridge, a rainforest-like canopy awaits with further trails and platforms among the trees.

Among Whistler and Grouse Mountain, the Capilano Suspension Bridge has cemented itself as a keystone of Canada's West Coast.
